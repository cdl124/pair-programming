Cortney's Guessing Game!

Here, via a series of questions for you to answer, we will learn a few things about the delightful Cortney Lane.

For "yes" or "no" questions, you may answer "yes", "y", "no", or "n".
For numeric-input answers, only write a number - no letters!

But first, the formalities - let's get your name down below...
